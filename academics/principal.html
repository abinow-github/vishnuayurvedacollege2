Dr Kirathamoorthy P.P is chairman of padartavignanam syllabus reform committee of National council of indian systems of medicine ,new delhi Dr Kirathamoorthy P.P, is currently working as vice principal of vishnu ayurveda college shornur.He is Professor and Hod in the Department of panchakarma also.He was working as professor and HOD of Samhitha and Sidhantha of VPSV Ayurveda College, Kottakkal . He Secured his UG from  Ayurveda College Coimbatore and PG in Kayachikitsa from Government College of Indian Medicine Mysore. He has a teaching experience of 37 years starting from 1986 to till date and guiding the PG scholars at VPSV Ayurveda College Kottakkal. He is the chief editor of quarterly journal sadvaidya , . Worked as co editor for the literature 'concept of mind', Member-Board of Studies, University of Calicut ,KUHS,and kannur University. Served as the chairman of board of exams for many times. Coordinator of Foreign studies, VPSV Ayurveda College and Duisburg University. He was Chief Editor of Bhishak News of vpsv ayurveda college kottakkal Member of Board of Studies (PG) KUHS.Member Secretary,VPSV Ayurveda College Council, Kottakkal. Guest lecturer- Hastya Ayurveda Course at Guruvayoor.
