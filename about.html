About Us

Home / About

Vishnu Ayurveda college was established in the year of 2002, located at Kulappully, Palakkad District, 4km away from the Shoranur railway station.It is situated in the premises of Vishnu Pharmaceuticals and Nursing home which was started in the year 1991.TheVishnu ayurveda college was made a reality by a great visionary Dr.P Ramachandran Nair. He is a renowned ayurveda practitioner and a great researcher.With a great futuristic vision, he is tirelessly working for acquiring high standards and quality of the organization. This college has been recognized by National Commission for Indian System of Medicine, under Ministry of AYUSH, Govt Of India.The college is affiliated to Kerala University of Health Sciences, Thrissur.Now Vishnu Ayurveda College is one among the best ayurveda colleges in India.

Rated as one among the best Ayurveda colleges affiliated to kuhs in terms of Infra structures and Facilities. Qualified, Experienced and Dedicated faculties Full fledged hospital with plenty of patients in all departments to support clinical practice. From these 2 decades 100 s of successful students are practising and promoting Ayurveda all over the world .
