Seminar Conducted - 2021

June 2019

On the occasion of international yoga day on 21 st June 2019 various programmes were organized in Vishnu Ayurveda college by the Swasthavritha department, college union and NSS unit. The program consisted of an Inter Ayurveda College yoga competition "YOGA DAKSHA", mass yoga demonstration and inauguration of yoga for women programme.

An Inter Ayurveda Yoga completion was conducted on 1 9th of june 201 9 in which 52 students participated from 6 colleges - Government Ayurveda College Thrippunithra, Nangeli Ayurveda College, Pariyaram Ayurveda college- Kannur, Asthanga Ayurveda college, Vaidyaratnam Ayurveda college Ollur and Vishnu Ayurveda College Shoranur. The competition included Shat Kriya and Yogasanas separately for boys and girls. Individual and team championship were separately conducted. Vishnu Ayurveda College and Government Ayurveda college Thrippunithura won the team championship for girls. Vishnu Ayurveda College won the team championship for boys. Dr Rakesh P of Vishnu Ayurveda college and Devika of Government Ayurveda College, Thrippunithura were declared as the individuals champions in the girls and boys section respectively. We were pleased to have Mr. Gopinathan, Smt Indu M V and Mr. K.K.Chandran as Judges for the competition.

November 2019

On the occasion of International Ayurveda Day, Dr. D. Sheela Professor, Department of Prasuti and Streeroga, conducted an awareness class in St. Theresa's Higher Secondary Girls School, Shoranur. On menstruation, allied problems and its mitigation through the wide spectrum of Ayurvedic knowledge. In connection with the shahla's accidental by snakebite death, Student's union and NSS unit of Vishnu Ayurveda College combined arranged an awareness class on "Snakebite - Revealing myths and facts" Dr. Jyothi Sajayan, Department of Agadatantra delievered the class.

February 2020

Awarness Program conducted to the public to prevent "Coranavirus" out break. Institutional program is planned on 6/2/2020 at college auditorium by Department of Swasthavrittam and NSS unit. Dr. Kailas, Government Hospital, Shoranur, delievered the class.

March 2020

On the occasion of "International Womens' Day" Celebration, EKA students union in association with NSS unit of Vishnu Ayurveda College is arranged an interactive session on 6/3/2020 in College Auditorium with Vimala Andarjanam is a successful Practitioner in Vishachikitsa.
