Our Campus

The college campus is having 10.73 acres of land on the main road side of Palakkad-Pattambi road - 1 KM to Kulappully bus stand and 4 KM to Shoranur bus stand and to Shoranur railway junction. The college building is having 6755.84 M 2 area to cater the entire needs of a college. The college is fully equipped to enhance the seats to 100 with gallery type class rooms with internet facilities. College Library is having 307.80 M 2 area with internet facility fully equipped with - all available Ayurvedic books and modern books- more than 10750 books. The college is having all facilities to start Post graduate degrees in all subjects of Ayurveda. The college factory for manufacturing of Ayurveda medicines is having a plinth area of 1551.68 M 2 equipped with all instruments/machineries – powdering machine 4, tablet punching machine, strip packing machine, Drier, pounding machine, grinding motors – ammi-14 numbers. The pharmacy/factory is having drug manufacturing license with GMP certificate, Excise licence, factory license, municipal license and all facilities to prepare all ayurveda preparations. In the last financial year pharmacy is manufactured medicine worth Rs. 5592413.55 and disbursed in the OP & IP for worth Rs. 3980369.55.

The college hospital is separate at the same time adjacent to the college. The hospital and college is connected with corridors, it will help to the students and teachers to go to the hospital and to bring the patient for demonstration purpose. Hospital building is having presently 3 floor with a plinth area of 3412.68 M 2 . 2 more floors – 3 rd & 4 th floor is nearing completion total 5652 M 2 .

The OP & IP is fully equipped with all facilities. The college is provided with lift and the hospital is also given provision for lift to the patients. However, other than two stair cases, ramp is provided from the ground to the top floor of the hospital building. The average bed strength in college hospital is 76+ . The requirement is 60 beds – college is maintaining 100 beds. The bed strength will be raised to 150 after occupying the third and fourth floor of the hospital building. College is having ladies hostel and boys hostel in separate buildings. Ladies hostel is 2817.18 M 2 in 5 story building. The building is having enough and more space to accommodate the students when the strength is increased to 100 and starting the PG in all subjects. Besides all the above, college is having a 50 beded well equipped allopathic hospital adjacent to the college campus – “Vishnu Sanjeevani Hospital” to support the entire needs of the college hospital from the allopathic side.

Presently the college is having BAMS course with intake of 60 students . The Government certificate course in Pharmacy (25 seats), Nursing (30 seats) and Masseur (30) seats are also functioning in this college. The total strength of BAMS students in 5 batches is 288. 34 Teaching Faculty Members are working in 14 departments. The Government of Kerala is given NOC to increase the BAMS seats from 60-100 and to start PG course in 4 subjects. CCIM/AYUSH was also prepared to sanction the same and waiting for the affiliations of the Kerala University of Health Sciences for final orders. The college is having more than 10 acres of land. All the medicinal plants are naturally grown in the compound. Besides this, a medicinal plants garden and a green house is functioning in the college to cater the entire needs of the students. The college is having a water treatment plant to treat the waste water. The waste water from hospital, factory and hostels are treated in this plant and it is used for the watering to the plants. The college is planning to cultivate, Navara and Rektasali Paddy to the patients. The management is planning to start a research centre within college compound. The home work of the building is going on. A most suitable place is earmarked for the building. The basic idea is rejuvenate the ‘Rasayana therapy’ without losing any basic principles behind it and to make use for ordinary people – not for making money and fame. It involves a lot of skill and money. Still management is on the way to achieve the goal.

The college is ensure water supply for whole year to avoid interference of the functioning of the hospital, hostel, factory etc. After completion of the building, college is planning to install Solar Electricity Unit. Even now solar water heater is in use, Rainwater harvesting is also will be introduced very soon.
